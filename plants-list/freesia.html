Freesia

Botanist Christian P. Ecklon decided to name the bloom after his friend, Dr. Freese as a symbol of their friendship. This is also why the flower's main meaning is now trust and friendship.

  • No part of the freesia is toxic to humans, and the highly scented blossoms can be used in salads rw or as a garnish.

  • Freesias symbolise different things depending on their colour. White - purity and innocence. Pink - motherly love. Yellow - joy. Red - passion.

  • Freesias are used in perfumes, lotions, shampoos, soaps, hand creams and other cosmetic products.

  • There are more than 1800 different types of Freesia spannig 16 species, each with its own unique colouring.
