Rose

Roses are versatile, come in a large array of shapes, sizes and colours, and are even edible!

  • Roses are one of the oldest flowers - they were used by the Romans to eat and flavour their drinks.

  • The rose is the national flower of the United States.

  • Many modern species of rose live 6 to 10 years, however some species will live to 50 years or more!

  • Roses are bred for different reasons - looks, smell or taste!
